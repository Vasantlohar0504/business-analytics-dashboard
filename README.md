## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover meaningful business insights.
Using **Python for data analysis**, **SQL for querying business metrics**, and **Power BI for visualization**, the project helps identify sales trends, customer behavior, and product profitability.

The goal is to transform raw sales data into **actionable insights for better business decision-making**.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* SQL
* Power BI

---

## 📂 Dataset

This project uses the **Sample Superstore Dataset**, which contains retail sales data including:

* Orders
* Customers
* Products
* Regions
* Sales
* Profit

This dataset helps simulate real-world retail business analysis.

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### Sales Trend Analysis

* Analyzed monthly and yearly sales trends
* Identified top-performing regions and products

### Customer Segmentation

* Analyzed sales by customer segments
* Identified high-value customers

### Profit Analysis

* Evaluated profit by product category and sub-category
* Identified loss-making products

### Regional Sales Performance

* Compared sales performance across regions
* Identified the most profitable markets

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Top Products
* Customer Segmentation

The dashboard allows users to explore business performance and trends interactively.
